\question{Prove that for some linear transform \(T : V \to W\) where \((\dim V) \in \Natural \) that we have  $\dim V = \nullity T + \rank T$}

Lets firstly note that:
\begin{align}
  \nullity T &\coloneqq \dim \kernel T \\ 
  \rank T &\coloneqq \dim \image T
\end{align}


Hence we re-write the original question as:
\begin{align}
  \dim V &= \nullity T + \rank T \\ 
  \dim V &= \dim \kernel T + \dim \image T 
\end{align}

\begin{align}
  n_T &= \dim \image T \\
